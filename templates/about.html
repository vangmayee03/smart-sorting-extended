About Nutrigaze

Nutrigaze is a smart tool that helps you check if your fruits and vegetables are fresh or rotten. Just upload a photo, and our technology will analyze it to give you an instant prediction.

Our goal is to reduce food waste and help everyone make healthier choices, ensuring confidence in the food you eat.
